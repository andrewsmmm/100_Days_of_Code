# 100_Days_of_Code

This is my repository for my projects from the Udemy course 100 Days of Code: The Complete Python Pro Bootcamp

I started my coding journey in April 2025 but gave up after about 4 weeks. The news of AI taking coding jobs made me despondent.
With the advent of AI how could I become a programmer?
In May 2026 I came back to Angela Yu's 100 Days of Python. This time (although my long-term goal may be to become a programmer) it is now more personal.
I want to build Python programs that help me with my marketing job (for example, build a program that can take a list of companies and look them up in RocketReach
and return names of people at those companies who are good targets for the company I work for). Also, I want to build an intelligence platform that can do market research
and find companies that might be good prospect targets. Plus, I am interested in using Python for robotics and another microcontroller applications.

My GitHub handle (Code-Odyssey) reflects the epic journey I am on. I don't know where it will land me but the journey through Python will take me places for sure.
